Important Helpline for Women

181

The Women Helpline (WHL) is a 24-hour confidential service for women and child survivors and victims of any form of violence, including domestic and intimate partner violence.

1091

The Women In Distress helpline is 1091. According to the policewomen, most calls they receive are from women suffering from domestic violence or facing abuses from in-laws.

112

The NCW Women Helpline is operated from the premises of the National Commission for Women, New Delhi. For any emergency assistance, you can call this number.

100

The call is answered by trained police personnel who are available 24/7. They listen to your emergency and assess the situation.
